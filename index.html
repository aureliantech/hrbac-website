Questions

Common questions

Q1
What if my patent application gets rejected at first?
Expect an initial rejection — it's a normal part of the process. We respond directly to the examiner and adjust the claims where needed.
Q2
How long does this take?
Timing depends on the patent office's own queue, which varies by technology area, and on how you want to approach it. If you need a patent number quickly — for a portfolio, funding round, or acquisition — I can push your application through as fast as the examiner's schedule allows. If there's no rush, we can take a more measured pace. Either way, we'll talk through the right approach for your situation.
